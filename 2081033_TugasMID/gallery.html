Favorite Heroes in Mobile Legend

Lancelot

Told, Lancelot comes from a Baroque family in the Land of Dawn. When he was still young, he showed a wonderful talent in fencing. Lancelot also received praise from people, but the praise that so much seemed to make him arrogant and forgetful.

When the Magical Tribes attack, Lancelot, having just woken up from last night's drinking party, arrogantly attacks them alone. After winning a few battles, he caught his guard and went after the rest of the Magician tribe's army alone. Shortly after that the Magician tribe that chose to retreat suddenly united and defeated Lancelot in the mountains. Lancelot also has a wound that makes him almost faint on the lake shore, until he finally meets the Queen of Windsor, Odette.

Lancelot was captivated by his chastity and realized how wrong he was to be arrogant and haughty in the past. While he was recuperating at Swan Castle, Lancelot immediately realized the magical potential of the Regina family, he could combine his fencing with the Shadow Step maneuver.

Lancelot's Skills

Lancelot will attack forward towards the target dealing 100 Physical Damage (+50 percent of the total Physical Attack), the target will be hit by Sword Mark. If the enemy hit does not have a Sword Mark, then Lancelot's Cooldown Skill 2 will be refreshed

Lancelot will show the Swordsmanship Technique within the area, the enemy may be hit a maximum of 3 times. Deals 250 Physical Damage (+150 percent Physical Attack). Then, enemies who are attacked 3 times in a row will be affected by a slow effect.

After a moment after Charge, Lancelot issues Executioner Strike towards the front, he will be Invisible or invisible during the process and deal 400 Physical Damage (+ 250 percent of total Physical Attack) to the enemy.
